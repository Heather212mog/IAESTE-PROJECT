IAESTE Kenya

IAESTE Kenya is a University Consortium of four universities, former Cooperating Institutions namely: IAESTE Kenya-JKUAT (Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology), IAESTE Kenya-DeKUT (Dedan Kimathi University of Technology), IAESTE Kenya-MUT (Murang’a University of Technology) and IAESTE Kenya-EU (Egerton University). The consortium was admitted as an Associate Member of IAESTE A.s.b.l. on 24th January 2021 during the Annual Conference in Vienna, Austria. Prior to this, the institutions undertook their exchange processes largely in an independent manner.

The Members of Iaeste-Kenya shall be the Local Committees (LCs) of the participating tertiary education institutions and industries. In exceptional circumstances, the association may consider individual membership subject to (i) affiliation to a tertiary educational institution, and (ii) payment of appropriate membership fee as shall be determined by the National Committee (NC).
